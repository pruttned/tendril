Tendril: Echo Received

Tendril: Echo Received is a 2D platformer where you are an evil demonic-like creature called Dragon.
Crawl on walls, through ventilation shafts, frighten civilians, hunt them down, modify their minds and bodies and get rid of those who are foolish enough to oppose you!

Inspiration

  • Aliens series – fluid wall movement and stealth
  • The Thing – DNA modifications
  • Cthulhu Mythos – something unimaginable is waiting to be woken from its eternal slumber

Features

  • Intuitive fluid control with crawling on all walls – just like a proper Alien would
  • Stealth – Silently hunt down your unsuspecting prey
  • Bio hacking – Modify your prey’s DNA to help
  • Live NPCs – Because hunt is more fun if they are scared
  • Awesome pixel art graphics
  • Gloomy cyberpunk with Art Deco look
